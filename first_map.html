FAVORITE PLACES IN MY HOMETOWN

Maracaibo, Venezuela

I grew up in Maracaibo Venezuela, the second largest city in the caribbean nation. Maracaibo is characterized for it's extremely warm weather, it's charismatic people, and for being the cradle of the oil industry in Venezuela. My favorite places in the town are my old school "Colegio Bellas Artes", the city's most famous park called "La Vereda del Lago", the botanical gardens, & Lago Mall which reminds me of my childhood.
